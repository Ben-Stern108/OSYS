Volunteer

OSYS is always looking for volunteers. Sign up now and help us run drop-in and recreational activities or develop and manage fundraisers and special events.

Types of Volunteering

Front-line Volunteer

Front-line volunteers work alongside staff to run drop-in and recreational activities. Whether it's handing out snacks, playing basketball, or helping to facilitate a workshop, you'll be directly working with youth. We are seeking people who are passionate about providing trauma-informed, nonjudgmental care. This is a weekly role, but we are flexible to work with your schedule!

Note: All frontline volunteers will be required to complete and pass a police check with a vulnerable sector check, along with an intervention check from Child and Family Services.

Project and Event Volunteering

Project and Event volunteers work on projects with a start and end date, or during special events. This role is great for people who have ideas for workshops, limited availability, or have a special passion that can benefit the youth who access services! Ideas in the past have been leading jam sessions, beading workshops, or serving food at our winter feast.

Have an idea but you're not sure it's a good fit? Fill out the form! We're always happy to workshop ideas with people.

Join Our Board!

The Board of Directors is the legal authority for the Old Strathcona
Youth Society. As a member of the Board, a Director acts in a position
of trust for the community and is responsible for the effective
governance of the organization. Board meetings are held every two
month and board members get to attend fundraisers and community
events throughout the year.
