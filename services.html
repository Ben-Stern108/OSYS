Our Services

OSYS provides a vast number of services to Edmonton's at-risk
youth. The services include essential services such as access to
transport and housing while also providing emotional and mental
health support.

To access our services, visit the OSYS location during the available
hours or book an appointment by calling the OSYS number.

Book an appointment: 780-496-5947

Essential Support Servives

ID Replacement

Having an ID can be an essential step to stability. OSYS can
take youth to the registry and pay for simple ID replacements,
replace AB Health Care Cards, and assist in birth certificate
replacements

Housing Info

Arc Cards

In partnership with the P.A.T.H. Program, we offer free ARC
Cards and bus tickets to those experiencing homelessness or
precarious housing. Please note, those on our ARC Card list
must check in with us every month before the 24th to get their
card reloaded!
