Donate

Every time you donate, you make a direct positive impact on
Edmonton's most vulnerable youth. We are beholden to the
community that helps us empower young people to take the first step
toward stability.

Donate Essential Items

We are always accepting in-kind donations!Please call us at
780-496-5947, or message us on Facebook or Instagram for
questions regarding donations, seasonal asks, or drop-off times!

We cannot accept food that has been opened, or prepared
outside a non-commercial facility, perishables, ripped or
unwashed clothing, open makeup, used socks and underwear, or
large home goods.

Fundraising Campaigns

Door-to-Door Coupon book sales

The Old Strathcona Youth Society has a marketing company doing door-to-door coupon book sales in the Edmonton, St. Albert, Sherwood Park, Spruce Grove, Leduc, Fort Saskatchewan and Stony Plain area. These are a fundraiser for the OSYS, and all funds raised from the purchases go towards staffing, recreation, and daily operating costs of the OSYS.
